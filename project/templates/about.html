About MediPredict AI

Learn about our AI-powered disease prediction system

Problem Statement

Early and accurate disease detection is critical for effective treatment. Many patients, especially in rural or under-served areas, lack immediate access to specialist doctors. This project aims to provide a preliminary AI-assisted diagnosis by analyzing user-reported symptoms and medical images (skin conditions, eye infections, tongue abnormalities, X-rays, and wounds). The system is designed as a decision-support tool — not a replacement for professional medical consultation.

How It Works
  1. Symptom Prediction: The user selects symptoms from a checklist. A Random Forest model (trained on 1,000 synthetic samples across 20 diseases) outputs the top-3 most probable diseases with confidence scores.
  2. Image Detection: The user uploads a JPG/PNG medical image. A deep learning model processes the image (resize → 224×224, normalize) and classifies it into one of 10 categories with high accuracy.
  3. Combined Analysis: Both outputs are fused with a weighted scoring algorithm (60% symptom, 40% image) to produce the final prediction.
